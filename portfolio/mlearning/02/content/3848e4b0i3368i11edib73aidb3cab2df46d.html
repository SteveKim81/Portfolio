​BLOOD PRESSURE

Is​ ​t​h​e​ ​m​e​a​s​u​r​e​m​e​n​t​ ​o​f​ ​p​r​e​s​s​u​r​e​ ​o​r​ ​f​o​r​c​e​ ​t​h​a​t​ ​p​u​s​h​e​s​ ​a​g​a​i​n​s​t​ ​t​h​e​ ​w​a​l​l​s​ ​o​f​ ​y​o​u​r​ ​a​r​t​e​r​i​e​s​.​

Is measured as systolic/diastolic mmhg (120/80mmHg).

​Systolic is the pressure when the heart pushes out blood to the body. 

Diastolic is the pressure in between when the heart is resting. 

A normal healthy blood pressure range is <120/80 mmHg. 

The units range is 100-180/50-59 mmHg.​
